Lemonade

The lemonade recipe makes avery refreshing drink to cool you down on a hot day

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups of white sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 9 medium lemons, or more as needed
  • 7 cups of ice-cold water
  • ice as needed

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Combine sugar and 1 cup water in a small sauceoan. Stir to dissolve sugar while misxture comes to aboil. Set aside to cool slightly.
  3. Meanwhile, roll lemons around on your counter to soften. Cut in half crosswise, and squeeze into a liquid measuring cup. Add pulp to the juice, but discard any seeds. Continue juicing until you have 1 Â½ cups fresh juice and pulp.
  4. Pour 7 cups ice-cold water into a pitcher. Stir in lemon juice and pulp, then add simple syrup to taste. Add ice. Fresh lemonade poured over ice in a glass pitcher.
  5. Enjoy!
